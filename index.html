About Me

I am a PhD Candidate at Renmin University of China advised by Prof. Xiaofeng Meng. Before that, I was a undergraduate student at School of Computer and Communication Engineering, China University of petroleum(East China) (UPC).

I am interested in data mining, including time series data and spatio-temporal data analytics. Besides, I interested in intelligent scientific discovery.
